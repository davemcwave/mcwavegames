Lethal – Press Kit

Lethal is a daily card puzzle game where you kill the enemy in one turn. Slay the Spire (card battler) meets Wordle (daily puzzles). Each puzzle has a unique set of cards and the player must play the cards in the correct order to reduce the enemy's health to 0. Play instantly in your browser for free at playlethal.fun. New puzzle every other day. Anyone can submit their own puzzles.
